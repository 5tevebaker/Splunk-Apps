Note

The Palo Alto Networks App (SplunkforPaloAltoNetworks) is supported via the Ask a Question button on the App homepage. A notification is sent to a team of technical representatives at Palo Alto Networks and Splunk, including the developers of the app, who will respond as soon as possible.

The Palo Alto Networks Add-On (Splunk_TA_paloalto) is supported by the same team, and also by Splunk Support. You can get support via the Ask a Question button, or by opening a ticket with Splunk.
